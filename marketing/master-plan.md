## Social Format Specs

### Stat Card Template
- Background: `#0A0A0B` with subtle gradient
- Metric: Instrument Serif, 96px, `#F5A623` (gold)
- Context: Inter, 24px, `#FFFFFF` (white)
- Logo: Partners in Biz wordmark (bottom right, 20% opacity)
- Font sizes scalable by platform (Instagram 72px, LinkedIn 96px)

### Quote Card Template
- Background: `#0A0A0B` with subtle gradient
- Quote: Instrument Serif, 48px, `#FFFFFF`, italic
- Attribution: Inter, 24px, `#A0A0A0` (muted)
- Company logo (if available): 48px square, bottom left
- Watermark: Partners in Biz wordmark (bottom right, 20% opacity)

### Comparison Card Template
- Background: Split down middle
- Left side (Status Quo): Dark gray gradient
- Right side (PiB): `#F5A623` gradient
- Labels: Inter, 32px, uppercase, bold
- Items: Inter, 24px, left-aligned, 2-3 points per side
- Arrow: Animated or static, pointing from left to right

## Production Handoff Notes

**Phase 3 Parallel Writers:**
- 8 blog writers (one per blog, with SEO keyword)
- 6 video composers (storyboards → HyperFrames → ffmpeg)
- 12-week social writer (7 posts per week, rotating formats)

**Phase 4 Image Generation:**
- Use Imagen 4.0 with master suffix
- Master suffix: "modern enterprise SaaS editorial style, clean corporate aesthetic, soft directional light, premium B2B product feel, deep navy/electric blue palette (or brand color), no people unless lifestyle is core, abstract or product focus, ultra-sharp details, photorealistic, no logos no text overlay"
- Generate all hero images + social card backgrounds
- Upload via `/api/v1/social/media/upload`

**Phase 5 Video Production:**
- Use bundled `scripts/generate-underscore.py` for music
- Use bundled `assets/composition-template.html` for HyperFrames
- Render all 3 formats (vertical, 16:9, 15s cut)
- Upload each format individually, register on single `social_post`

**Approval Workflow:**
- All content goes to `/portal/campaigns/[id]` for client review
- Client can approve, request changes, or edit inline
- After approval, bulk schedule via `POST /api/v1/campaigns/[id]/schedule`
